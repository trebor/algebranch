## How to sign

Algebranch uses an automated CLA check on pull requests (CLA Assistant). The first
time you open a pull request, a bot will comment with instructions and a link. To
sign, **post the comment it asks for on your pull request** (typically:

> I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA

). Your signature — your GitHub username and the date — is recorded in this
repository, and the CLA status check on your pull request turns green.

- **Individuals:** sign as yourself.
- **Corporate contributors:** have an authorized representative sign the CCLA, and
  ensure each contributing employee is on your designated-employee list. Contact
  the Project Maintainer (via a GitHub issue) to arrange the corporate signature.

You only need to sign once; the signature covers your present and future
Contributions to the Project.
